About Reindeers

Reindeer, or caribou, are primarily plant eaters that love
feeding on leafy greens and mushrooms, and on occasion, bird
eggs and arctic char. And though they're not available to them
in their natural habitat, they also love carrots and apples as a little sweet treat.
